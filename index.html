Hello!

I am an econometrician at Rokos Capital Management. I was previously working as a senior economist at the Research Department of the Central Bank of Malta, and as an economist at the European Central Bank's Monetary Policy Strategy department. My research interests include monetary policy, time series--econometrics, and forecasting.
